In 2.11.0 version of the modpack Angelica (the optimization mod) is having compatibility issues with Xaero maps.

That means that in the 2.11 the Xaero map will be disabled until the next patch update (2.11.X).
In the mods folder you can find an older version of Angelica that still works with Xaero if you need it.

As an alternative to Xaero, there is Mapwriter (https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/mapwriter-2) you could use.
